StaticLess™

不同于传统服务器，无需管理服务器基础设施

静态服务器（Static-Server）帮助开发人员快速原型化应用程序，降低成本和复杂性。

我们曾认为 Serverless 是为了让开发人员能够快速原型化产品并展示结果，而不需要过多地关注服务器部署和相关问题。

事实上， Serverless 在实践中与这些概念是一致的，然而，它仍然没有达到我们最初超低成本和卓越易用性的愿景。

StaticLess 利用基于静态网页的服务器模拟技术，具有超低成本和极易上手的潜力，非常适合需要快速开发原型和服务器要求很低的项目。

We once perceived Serverless as a means for developers to rapidly prototype products and demonstrate results without needing to concern themselves excessively with server deployment and related issues.

Indeed, Serverless aligns with these concepts in practice; however, it has yet to fulfill our initial vision of ultra-low costs and exceptional ease of use.

StaticLess, leveraging server emulation technology based on static web pages, holds the potential for ultra-low costs and exceptional ease of adoption, making it highly suitable for projects requiring rapid prototyping and minimal server requirements.
